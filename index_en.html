The future in practice

Something more than a school, something different to an academy. A place of training which is already a workplace, where you learn by doing. Where the teachers are professionals and entrepreneurs who apply and renew the business knowledge and experience that they impart on a daily basis. Whether you feel more like a startupper or dream of being employed, our goal is to broaden the field of your possibilities. Campus, in fact. The ITS Foundation for ICT Piemonte is an incubator for the future.

The field of action

The Piazza dei Mestieri. A complex of industrial archaeology converted into classrooms with interactive whiteboards, an Apple room with 50 iMacs and a PC room with 90 workstations, study rooms in which to develop individual and team projects, a meeting hall, outdoor areas where to mix socially, and the Piazza’s brewery, which hosts events and concerts. The tools and means of production are available to students, from tablets to cameras, which can also be borrowed to continue work and experimentation off-campus.

Mobile App Design

Senior Technician in methods and technologies for the development of software systems.

Apps are here to stay. If you can think of them and create them, however, you’ll never stop. The two-year Mobile App Design course covers the entire production cycle of applications, from concept to development, from execution to business model. You will acquire the necessary skills to conceive an aesthetically and functionally complete multimedia product, design an application from specific data, develop and test the mobile apps, distribute and promote them, and to move knowledgeably and effectively in the workplace and in business contexts. We don’t just train programmers, but real designers.

Videomaking

Diploma of Higher Level Technician in the organization and use of information and knowledge.

More screens, everywhere and always available = infinite programme schedules to fill = a huge demand for audiovisual content to satisfy. The two-year Videomaking course gives you the knowledge to allow you to tune in to these needs. At the end of the course you will know how to conceive, design and create a quality audiovisual product, create 2D and 3D graphics and animations, work in both the creative and technical worlds in any situation in which content is produced, distributed or consumed, and to move knowledgeably and effectively in the world of work and in business contexts. The course provides a common basis and two alternative and complementary specializations: 3D animation and video post-production.

It's time to go back to school

It is the very nature of the campus of ITS for ICT Piemonte - common ground between education and the world of work - which makes it the ideal place where companies can meet and prosper, recruit young talent, and help ideas and innovation to germinate..
Browse these pages to find out all the possibilities for collaboration, and contact us on 011 0371500 or at info@its-ictpiemonte.it to discuss your requirements.

Plug-and-play talents

Competence, enthusiasm, courage and an irrepressible desire to do: if you are looking for thinking minds and new energies to integrate into your company, the young people of the ITS campus for ICT Piemonte are the solution. On our courses you will find interaction designers, coders, graphic designers, experts in the “internet of things”, directors, editors, 2D and 3D animators, sound technicians, and much more. We find the right formula together: you can host students in work experience for more than three months, and, naturally, look for people to employ under contract.

When we say ‘invest in training’

Sharing knowledge and experience in a training project for young professionals is a real opportunity for a company to test and reprogram its vision and capabilities, create authority and increase its visibility to institutions and stakeholders, to network and meet new potential partners. The campus is open to different ways of working together, from the inclusion of teachers in didactic modules to the creation of talks and themed workshops.

We design together

Our watchword: co-creation. You can mobilize the creative energy of the participants in the courses on campus by offering briefs designed for the needs of your company. The project is evaluated and then inserted into the educational cycle and the students, led by a close team of teachers and involving the partners in the process, will work to find the most innovative ideas and the most effective solutions. The duration of projects can range from the very short term, such as 15 days, up to three months or more.
